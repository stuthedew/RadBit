Radiation bean

Wearable Radiation sensor based off of LightBLue Bean and Teviso RD2007

A library for operating RadBean Fitbit-style radiation tracker that interfaces LightBlue Bean with Teviso RD2007 radiation sensor and 256K FRAM memory.
